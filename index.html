G. DAVID MacGREGOR

Grew up on ranches in the mountains of central Idaho. He spent much of his youth in logging camps and on cattle ranches. Listening to some real "characters" spin yarn. His working life has taken him to many remote places His happiest days were lookinf afteer his cows in the high mountais with his quarter horses and Australian shepherds. He developed an appetite for storytelling after working all around the northern hemisphere, in rural places, with characters of diverse nationalities, his imagination grew and the 5 book "Conifer Cliffs" series was born.

He now spend his time with his wife June, between Valley Country, Idaho and the Bighorn mountains in Wyoming.

Conifer Cliffs Book Series

Conifer Cliffs is a realistic, historical fiction, packed with mystery and suspense that will keep you enthralled and wanting more.
This series is a legacy you will want to read and a story we can all aspire to.

Inspirations

Imagine growing up in a wide spread community knowing everyone form bankers, lawyers, cattle barons, lumber tycoons, and founders of national grocery store chains. And on the other side of the spectrum colorful personalities of the loggers, cowboys, construction workers, and all the people whose work contributed to the wealth of the movers and shakers. Then, we cannot forget those who drug their feet in the sand, slowing society to circumvent progress, the drunks, druggies, thieves and scammers.
